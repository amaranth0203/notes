等待程序执行完毕再继续执行:
echo before ping
start /wait ping www.baidu.com -n 2 
echo after ping

根据程序执行返回的结果来进行分支:

echo before ping
start /wait ping aaa -n 2
IF ERRORLEVEL 1 GOTO errorHandling
echo after ping
pause > nul
goto :EOF
:errorHandling
echo ping failed
pause > nul
